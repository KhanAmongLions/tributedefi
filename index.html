  • The top 50 TRBT holders will continuously receive free TRBT reward drops directly to their wallet, without having to do anything!

  • 11. Will the Uniswap liquidity be locked?
    Yes, this will happen soon after the token is released to establish a fully trustless system.
  • 12. What are the developer fees?
    The developers will be given 5% of the token supply only. Nothing else.
  • 13. Will TRBT have any dapps?
    Yes, the developing team will launch the token with two fully functioning dapps that were designed to fully benefit the players and TRBT ecosystem together. More dapps will be built with time and launched on the platform. The third dapp that will be released has a very unique concept and has never been done before with any token. This will definitely be one to hold your TRBT for.
  • 14. I am a top 50 TRBT holder but I didn’t get any reward drop?
    The reward drops are completely random. A top 50 wallet is rewarded by random choice. How many times you receive a reward drop are completely dependent on luck and statistics. Also take note, that if you are staking your TRBT in the conTRIBUTE dapp then your TRBT is not actually sitting within your wallet even though you own these tokens. So in this case, despite holding a top 50 position your wallet does not reflect this. Therefore, it is up to you to weigh the potential benefits of either holding your TRBT in your wallet for rewards vs staking it in in the conTRIBUTE dapp for passive dividends. Both methods are rewarding, but one is more constant and the other is more spread out.
  • 15. Does the conTRIBUTE dapp have any referral rewards?
    Yes, referrals receive 1% of all entry fees from the people they refer as direct dividends, from the total 5% entry fee.
  • 16. What is the Community Dapp?
    This will be a dapp developed by request of the TRIBUTE community. Any request will be set forth as a final vote by the entire community and made as requested. This is done in spirit of the full collaboration of a decentralized community that has full access to contribute in the growth of the TRIBUTE ecosystem. Moving forward, the community will always have a say in the developments of TRIBUTE.
  • 17. Can I develop dapps for TRBT?
    Absolutely. Anyone can develop a dapp that uses TRBT. In fact, we can even whitelist your dapp from the transfer Burn’n’Reward function if it is required for your dapp to function optimally.

  • 2. How do I buy/sell on Uniswap?
    You must make sure to set your slippage to at least 10% when buying/selling this token, otherwise your transaction might fail due to the Burn’n’Reward associated with your transfer. Do this on the top right of the uniswap page by clicking the settings gear and typing in the slippage you desire.
  • 3. What wallet do I need to buy/sell/hold TRBT?
    Any ERC20 compatible wallet will work. Metamask, Trust Wallet and MyEtherWallet are currently the most popular options available.
  • 4. Where does the value for TRBT come from?
    The value of the token is derived from market supply and demand. The aggressive burning that is associated with token transfers will add a powerful upward momentum on the price due to the constant decrease of total supply of tokens. With ETH liquidity remaining the same, each token will be worth more ETH with every transfer on the ecosystem.
  • 5. How is the random address selected to receive the reward from transfers?
    The random address reward function is done through a complex RNG on the smart contract. This mechanism is fully automated and cannot be manipulated. It will randomly pick one address from the top 50 TRBT holders to receive the reward. This happens for every single transfer of TRBT tokens, no matter how big or small.
  • 6. Should I hold a lot of TRBT?
    You are encouraged to always be a top 50 TRBT holder, so as to receive as many reward drops as possible. Some rewards will be much bigger than others and you never know when your address might be selected to receive the reward.
  • 7. How do I know if I received a reward drop?
    You will be able to see your token amount increase immediately, and you can also check your ERC20 wallet or etherscan page to see your TRBT deposits. You can also track your reward drops on our website.
  • 8. What is a token burn?
    A token burn is when ERC20 tokens are sent to the Ethereum burn address. This address cannot be accessed by anyone and is essentially a burial ground for tokens to go where they are forever removed from circulation. You can follow the tokens to verify that they have been burnt.
  • 9. Will TRBT have an airdrop?
    No, the entire token supply will be used from genesis to fund the Uniswap liquidity as well as 2 dapps. A small OTC was done to supply ETH for the Uniswap liquidity pool, and a small supply is reserved for the developers and marketing.
  • 10. What is the TRBT OTC?
    OTC stands for Over the Counter. It is in essence, a presale of sorts. The reason an OTC was done for TRBT was to collect a small amount of ETH to supply to Uniswap as liquidity upon listing. This will stabilize the price and give a more even distribution to those who buy the token. The team does NOT receive these funds.

**CONTRIBUTE DAPP***

There are many variations of staking dapps in the crypto community, and after participating in a wide variety of them we have come up with the optimal structure for the offical TRIBUTE staking dapp.

This is a dapp that was designed to reward TRIBUTE holders and conTRIBUTE players in a truly synergistic method. A conventional hourglass contract was used as the basis for which this staking dapp was developed. However, the hourglass code was modified in its structure to make it non-zero-sum. This is a new idea, as most hourglass dapps are by their nature zero-sum.

  • 5% ENTRY FEE.
  • 15% EXIT FEE.
  • Stable non-fluctuating price.
  • 10% of the initial TRBT token supply (50,000 TRBT) has been withheld from circulation and assigned as exclusive rewards for conTRIBUTE holders.
  • The 50,000 TRBT will be periodically injected into conTRIBUTE as direct dividends to all the players.
  • The longer you hold your TRBT in this dapp, the more dividends you will be guaranteed to make. This dapp is aimed at players who prefer a constant stream of passive income.
  • All transfers into and out of the dapp will not be subject to the 5% burn or the 5% reward drop, meaning there will be no transfer fee as is typically associated with all transfers of TRBT on the blockchain.
  • The dapp contains no developer fee. All funds are 100% distributed to players.
  • Your TRBT sent to this contract is not eligible for the 5% reward drops, because it will no longer be in your wallet and you will therefore not be considered a top 50 holder while staking.
  • You recieve 20% of the entry fee as direct dividends from anyone who uses your custom referral link.
  • Playing the game has a positive effect on token price by locking liquidity.
  • The dapp is designed to punish sellers as they exit their positions, but holders will be rewarded by accumulating substantial dividends from any exits.

Enforce Dapp Information

This is a new concept dapp that has never been done before, designed in a way to enforce burns and rewards of TRBT on a daily basis. A smart contract was built which can be seeded with TRBT tokens only. The tokens within this contract can never be withdrawn or sold.

The Enforced TRIBUTE smart contract will be seeded from its genesis with 50,000 TRBT tokens, equating to 10% of the total supply.

  • Click the button to trigger a Burn'n'Reward function.
  • The Burn'n'Reward trigger can only be called once every 12 hours.
  • The function will carry out a transfer of 10,000 TRBT between its in-built contracts.
  • This triggers the autonomous burning of 500 TRBT and a reward drop of 500 TRBT to a random wallet in the top 50 holders.
  • This see-saw function will continue forever until the Enforced TRIBUTE smart contract runs out of TRBT completely, meaning the initial seed of 50,000 TRBT will be completely used for token burns and rewards.
  • The dapp will continue to function until it has depleted it's TRBT seed, meaning a total of 25,000 TRBT will have been burnt and 25,000 TRBT will have been distributed to holders as reward drops.
  • Enforced TRIBUTE is a powerful driving force made to push up the price of TRBT daily by making the supply even more scarce.
